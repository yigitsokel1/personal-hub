## Experiment

I mapped draft summaries to nearest existing tags using embedding similarity and simple thresholding.

## Observation

Editors accepted suggestions more often when examples of past usage were shown next to each candidate tag.

## Takeaway

Assistive recommendations are useful, but final selection must remain human-reviewed to avoid taxonomy drift.
