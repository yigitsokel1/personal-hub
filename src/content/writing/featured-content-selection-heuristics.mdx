## Problem

Featured sections lose signal when every good item is marked as featured.

## Heuristics

Select entries that maximize:

- recency of current capability
- diversity of domain signal
- quality of explanatory depth

## Practical rule

Keep featured sets small and rotate only with intent, not on every publish.

## Result

The homepage becomes a trust signal, not a noisy list of everything available.
