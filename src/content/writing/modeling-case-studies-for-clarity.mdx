## Premise

Case studies are often written as one-off stories. That makes them hard to maintain once you need tags, archives, and metadata consistency.

## Better approach

Split content into two layers:

- structured frontmatter for reusable facts
- narrative MDX body for context and judgment

## Benefits

This model keeps list pages reliable while preserving long-form depth on detail pages.

## Closing

Treat case studies as maintained product assets, not static marketing snapshots.
