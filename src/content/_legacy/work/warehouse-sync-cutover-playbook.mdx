## Problem

Batch windows were too slow for operating decisions, but changing the sync model introduced risk across analytics and planning teams.

## Method

We treated cutover as a product rollout: parallel runs, divergence thresholds, and explicit rollback criteria.

## Result

The organization adopted faster metrics without sacrificing trust in data correctness.
