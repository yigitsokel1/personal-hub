## Context

The onboarding funnel was business-critical but brittle. A single transient failure could strand users in ambiguous states.

## Execution

We reworked API write paths for idempotency and introduced retry semantics that reflected real-world dependency behavior.

## Outcome

The flow became resilient enough for growth experiments without repeated operational fire drills.
