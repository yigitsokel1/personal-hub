## Objective

The goal was to reduce stale content windows without over-invalidation.

## Approach

I compared broad revalidation with targeted tag invalidation across controlled publish bursts.

## Interim conclusion

Targeted invalidation is promising, but batching strategy is required for predictable origin cost.
